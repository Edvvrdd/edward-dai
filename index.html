More About My Work

I am a technical sound designer with a unique perspective shaped by completing multiple games independently across the full development lifecycle. This hands-on experience across programming, design, and audio has given me deep insight into how sound design integrates with every stage of production, enabling me to collaborate effectively with engineers, designers, and artists while anticipating technical requirements and creative opportunities.

My expertise centers on creating interactive audio systems using FMOD, Wwise, and game engines including Unreal Engine and Unity. I specialize in designing responsive, data-driven soundscapes that adapt dynamically to player actions and environmental factors. Beyond implementation, I approach sound design as world-buildingâ€”crafting emotionally resonant audio experiences through careful attention to subtle details that enhance immersion. I believe great game audio shouldn't just sound impressive in isolation; it should make players feel fully present in the game world.

My technical knowledge allows me to push beyond traditional linear sound design, creating highly interactive and detailed audio that responds intelligently to gameplay. From field recording and original sound creation to optimizing audio pipelines and integrating middleware, I prioritize developing scalable solutions that serve both the creative vision and the practical needs of small to mid-sized teams. I am particularly drawn to studios where sound plays a defining role in the game's identity, and where experimentation and close collaboration are valued throughout the creative process.
