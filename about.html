Hello! My name is Edward Dai, and I’m a sound designer and game audio enthusiast. I specialize in creating immersive soundscapes and interactive audio that enhance storytelling, gameplay, and user experience.

With a background in both music and game development, I bring a unique perspective to audio design. My approach combines technical precision with creativity — from sculpting subtle ambiences that bring environments to life, to designing impactful sound effects that make gameplay more engaging.

My focus areas include:

  • Game audio implementation (Unity, Wwise, FMOD)
  • Sound design for interactive media and games
  • Music composition and production
  • Audio editing, mixing, and mastering

When I’m not working on sound, I enjoy exploring game design, experimenting with new music production techniques, and discovering ways that technology and art can connect to create memorable experiences.

If you’d like to collaborate, please don’t hesitate to reach out through my contact page.
