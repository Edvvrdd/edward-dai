## Design Philosophy

The Dark Souls 3 palette is characterized by:
- **Extremely dark, desaturated backgrounds** creating a somber, atmospheric feel
- **Subtle warm (copper) and cool (blue) accents** that provide visual interest without being vibrant
- **Low contrast text** that's readable but maintains the dark aesthetic
- **Layered depth** through slightly different shades of dark grays
- **Medieval/gothic atmosphere** perfect for game audio portfolios

This palette works particularly well for:
- Horror/dark fantasy game portfolios
- Audio design showcases
- Atmospheric, immersive experiences
- Professional yet artistic presentations
