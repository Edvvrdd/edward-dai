## Instructions for Use

1. **Fill in the Metadata** at the top with your post's date, categories, and image details
2. **Replace all bracketed placeholders** `[like this]` with your actual content
3. **Add or remove sections** as needed for your specific post
4. **Convert to HTML** using the `blog-template.html` file as a reference
5. **Save your HTML file** with a descriptive name (e.g., `your-project-name.html`)
6. **Add your featured image** to the `images/blog/` directory
7. **Update the blog listing page** (`blog.html`) to include a link to your new post

### Styling Tips
- Use `<em>` tags for *italicized text*
- Use `<strong>` tags for **bold text**
- Use `<h2>` for main sections
- Use `<h3>` for subsections
- Use `<ul>` and `<li>` for bullet lists
- Keep paragraphs concise and readable
