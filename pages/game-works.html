Personal Project

Galaga Remake

A personal project inspired by the Sound Design discord's January Challenge. The original prompt was a simple video but I took it a step further by recreating the game from scratch and hooking it up to FMOD. I released the project as a free open learning project for others to try out their own sounds in the game.

Role: Team Lead, Programmer, Technical Sound Designer
Tools: Wwise, Unity
Duration: 4.5 months
